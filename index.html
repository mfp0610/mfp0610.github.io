About Me


I am a fourth-year undergraduate student at Huazhong University of Science & Technology (HUST), working for a B.Eng. in Automation in the School of Artificial Intelligence & Automation (AIA). I work closely with Prof. Kun He.

Currently, I am a member of Image and Video Understanding Laboratory (IVUL), Visual Computing Center, King Abdullah University of Science & Technology, working with Prof. Bernard Ghanem, seeking for potential Ph.D. opportunities there.
